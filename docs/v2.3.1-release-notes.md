# Release Notes — v2.3.1 — AI Decision Ledger & Runtime Governance

Adds the new **AI Decision Ledger & Runtime Governance** module to provide comprehensive tracking, forensic auditing, and operational compliance under ISO/IEC 42001-inspired frameworks.

## 🌟 What's New in v2.3.1

* **AI Decision Ledger Module** — Created a forensic ledger page (`/decision-ledger`) tracking high-velocity synthetic operational AI decisions, human approvals, policy outcomes, and evidence traces.
* **Drill-down Forensic Detail Panel** — Logs audit trail details, applied policy checks, triggered controls, risk rationales, and tamper-evident signatures for each decision event.
* **Bilingual EN/ES Experience** — Fully localized in English and Spanish mapped dynamically via standard UI contexts.
* **Public Demo Validation Checklist** — Authors `docs/demo-validation.md` outlining rigorous manual checklists for testing public deployment robustness.
* **Syncs & Upgrades** — Syncs versioning across package assets, updates README badges and module maps, and registers navigation links under the sidebar.
* **Premium Assets** — Bundled a high-impact responsive interface mockup of the Decision Ledger under `docs/screenshots/12-decision-ledger.png`.
